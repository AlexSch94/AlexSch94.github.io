Rail Logistics

The requirements for the operation and maintenance of freight cars are diverse and demanding. From scheduling wheelset maintenance to monitoring transport progress in international traffic, complying with safety and maintenance regulations and maximizing wagon availability are major challenges.

DOT Telematik und Systemtechnik GmbH offers a comprehensive, modular management system consisting of mobile telematics devices and data acquisition sensors, which feed their information into the DOT backend services, where the data gets processed, analysed, and enriched, providing you with the insights you need to overcome operational and logistical challenges. The devices are specifically developed for challenging rail operations and enable autonomous, maintenance-free operation.

Container Logistics

Keeping track of containers during their journey across the globe is a notoriously time-consuming job. The X-Rayl product line finally allows streamlining and simplifying this process through the use of comprehensive telematic and analytic capabilities.

The X-Rayl Solar Pointer monitors the departure of a transport, reports the entry and duration of stay in ports or terminals, detects the crane loading process and the arrival at both intermediate stations and the destination. In the event of excessive idle time at a transfer station, the responsible dispatcher is automatically alerted. This not only shortens turnaround times, but also reduces operational overhead by eliminating the time spent on follow-up phone calls.

Even remotely monitoring temperature sensitive cargo, with extensive notification and alarm options is just one of the many benefits of the X-Rayl system. The system pays for itself within a few months, thanks to the savings in rental and personnel costs.

Key Advantages

Turnaround monitoring

Turnaround time optimisation

Complete and constant overview of all transports

Fleet management control

Rental cost savings

Specific data on entry / departure times and loading time

Building Energy Management

When managing a commercial building, losing track of any operational parameters of power systems, heating, ventilation, and mechanical equipment can severely impact costs. The upkeep of large buildings such as offices, shopping centers and gas stations can be unnecessarily expensive due to superfluous heating and cooling, excessive downtimes of equipment and redundant lighting.

Thanks to DOT's comprehensive line of wireless telematics devices and advanced data analytics capabilities, monitoring a building has never been easier.

Once the sensory equipment is mounted, high frequency measurements and wireless transmission into the DOT backend software allow for analysis of every aspect of your building, without the need for external power supply.

Key Advantages

Save costs

Control temperature

Control lighting

Automated notifications in case of deviations

Reduction of personnel costs
