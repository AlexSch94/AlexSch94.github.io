Rail Logistics

The requirements for the operation and maintenance of freight cars are diverse and demanding. From scheduling wheelset maintenance to monitoring transport progress in international traffic, complying with safety and maintenance regulations or maximizing wagon availability are major challenges.

DOT offers a comprehensive, modular management system consisting of mobile telematics devices and sensors that provide data acquisition capabilities and feed their information into the DOT backend services, where the data gets processed, analysed and enriched to provide you with the insights you need to overcome operational and logistics challenges. The telematics devices are specially developed for demanding rail operations and enable autonomous, maintenance-free operation.

Container Logistics

Keeping track of containers during their journey across the globe used to be a time-consuming job, that is now easier than ever thanks to comprehensive telematics and analytics capabilities provided by DOT Telematik und Systemtechnik GmbH.

The X-Rayl Solar Pointer monitors the departure of a transport, reports the entry and the duration of stay in ports or terminals, detects the crane loading and the arrival at the destination or intermediate stations. In the event of excessive idle time at a transfer station, the responsible dispatcher is automatically alerted. This not only shortens turnaround times, but also reduces operational overhead by eliminating the time spent on follow-up phone calls.

Even remotely monitoring temperature sensitive cargo, with extensive notification and alarming options is just one of the many benefits of the X-Rayl system. The system pays for itself within a few months thanks to the savings in rental and personnel costs.

Property Energy Management

When managing a commercial building, losing track of all the operational parameters of power systems, heating, ventilation, and mechanical equipment can severely impact costs. Due to unnecessary heating or cooling, excessive downtimes of equipment or unnecessary lighting the cost of running a large building like an office, shopping center or gas station can be unnecessarily high.

Thanks to DOT's comprehensive line of wireless sensor and telematics devices, as well as advanced data analytics capabilities, monitoring a building has never been easier. Just mount the sensor equipment exactly where needed, and due to high frequency measurements and wireless transmission into the DOT backend software, every aspect of your building is now yours to be analysed, no external power required.
