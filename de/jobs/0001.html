Full Stack Developer

Hiring effective immediately, for our location in Leobendorf close to Vienna.

What are your areas of responsibility?

You will be responsible for the development, realisation, and validation of software systems in the field of telematics, with a focus on real-time streaming data architectures. These ensure high throughput and low latency for the processing of real-time data feeds.

Specifically, for the following tasks:

  • Definition of new products with the management and the development team

  • Participation in the product management process

  • Requirement analysis

  • Development of new data analysis functionalities

  • Design and implementation of new software systems according to the requirement analyses

  • Validation, maintenance, and upkeep of the back-end system, especially with regard to performance under the continuous load of 100 000+ telematics devices

Which criteria should you fulfil?

  • Academic degree or equivalent technical education or experience with IT focus (e.g. HTL/FH)

  • Solid experience of the Java programming language

  • Experience in the programming languages Go, C++ is an advantage

  • Experience with Apache Kafka is an advantage

  • Experience with containerisation is an advantage

  • Experience in the connection and creation of interfaces (REST, SOAP)

  • Knowledge of SQL and NoSQL format JSON, Elasticsearch is an advantage

  • Knowledge in web development PHP/JavaScript, CSS and HTML is an advantage

  • Knowledge in the use of Git, Bitbucket and Jira is an advantage

Which personality would fit the team?

We are looking for a team player, who is used to working in an independent and solution-oriented manner. Commitment and flexibility, as well as quality consciousness are major assets for our team.

What do we offer?

The well-being of our employees is important to us - flexible working hours, opportunities to learn new skills and to develop further, as well as a pleasant working environment in a sustainable family business offer many advantages. Working conditions are adapted to your needs, allowing for a healthy life-work balance.

You can expect a diverse range of tasks in connection with the latest technologies, independent and task-oriented work with the opportunity to help shape a young and dynamic team.

A modern workplace in a green area in an energy self-sufficient office building, only 25 minutes away from Vienna by express train.

For above-average performance by qualified applicants, the gross annual salary is above average.
