Easy assignment of assets and pairing of devices.

Easy Setup

The X-Rayl Scanner app allows for both quick and easy pairing of X-Rayl Sensors to X-Rayl Pointers and assigning the devices to assets via preexisting QR codes and asset numbers. Furthermore, assets can be assigned to plaques, also by scanning the asset number and QR code.

Before using the Scanner for mobile devices for pairing, the user is required to authenticate via a code or signing sign in. All transmissions between the app and the DOT server are encrypted.

The X-Rayl Scanner app is available for Android™ 5.0 or higher and iOS® Version 11.0 or higher in the respective App store.

Find the app for Android™ here, or search for "X-Rayl Scanner" in the Apple Store for the iOS version.
