X-Rayl® DOT-Link

The DOT-Link provides full insight into the state of your operation at the tip of your fingers.

Complete Access

Our DOT-Link enables constant access to the position and status of the assets and cargo of your fleet, improving operational cost and time-efficiency.

Seamless integration of the X-Rayl System, allows effortless scaling with the growth of your deployment.

Ever evolving to fit modern-day requirements, it demonstrates the future potential of logistical solutions.

Accessibility

The DOT-Link Portal delivers all the information on your asset straight to you. Since it is accessible on any browser, you are never more than one click away.

Tracking

DOT-Link makes it easy to track your X-Rayl system. Additionaly, geofencing technology allows you to freely determine regions and borders, triggering live notifications when they are crossed.

Visualisation

All data, including the route, temperature, shock reports, door openings and many more get displayed to you in comprehensive charts and graphs.

Security

Naturally, all data sent and recieved via the X-Rayl DOT-Link is encrypted using sophisticated algorithms, ensuring privacy and security for your operation.

Customisability

Multiple individually configurable alerts, filters and classifications allow you to fine tune the software to your own needs.

Optimisation

Analysis of logistic streams via real-time data feeds, route statistics, event history and more facilitates optimal usage of your asset or fleet components, at any given time.
