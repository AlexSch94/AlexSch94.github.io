X-Rayl® Sensor S3MEP-D

Detects Contact

The X-Rayl Sensor S3MEP-D is a contact sensor, developed to detect the presence of ferromagnetic metals.

From monitoring door states to assessing the lock state of a King Pin - the use cases for the S3MEP-D sensor are numerous.

The maximum detection distance is 5mm for ferrous metals and can be increased to 20mm by employing neodymium magnets (NdFeB) on the detected surface.

The X-Rayl Sensor S3MEP-D is equipped with a super-low-power TFT-LCD Panel, which allows for easy evaluation of the contact state.

Maintenance free

All X-Rayl Sensors are designed to be 100 percent maintenance free over their lifetime of 10+ years. No battery changes or charging is required, activation and deactivation as well as configuration is done automatically or over the air.

Highest certifications

All X-Rayl Sensors are certified according to ATEX, UKEX and IECEx.
It conforms with the standards:
EN IEC 60079-0:2018 / EN 60079-11:2012
Category: II 2G Ex ib IIC T6 Gb / II 2D Ex ib IIIC T85°C Db /
-40°C ≤ Ta ≤ +60°C.

Easy to mount

All X-Rayl Sensors S3 are easy to mount due to a variety of available mounting plates. In case of special mounting requirements individual mounting plates can be created according to customer specifications.

Security

From the data transmission to the X-Rayl Pointer to relaying of the data to the DOT backend services, the data generated by all X-Ray Sensors is always transmitted with strong industry standard encryption.

Large display

The large LCD display allows users to easily verify the values measured by the sensor when standing in front of the asset the sensor is mounted on. This allows on site personnel to monitor sensor values and verify the operation of the sensor.

Lightweight

The X-Rayl Sensors S3 weigh only 280 grams and have a very small form factor allowing for installation in places where regular hardware might be too heavy (e.g. pipes)

High sampling frequency

By default, the X-Rayl Sensors S3 are configured to sample data at 10 second intervals. On board difference and gradient detection allow for reduced sample transmission in case the measured value has not changed since the last measurement, saving energy and data.

No service fee

The operation of X-Rayl Sensors is free of charge, in combination with an X-Rayl Pointer. When pairing third party sensors with X-Rayl Pointers, a service charge may apply.

Future proof

All DOT devices have the option of over-the-air firmware updates, which ensures that our customers benefit from new features, improved reliability, and maximum security, all remotely and without physical contact. The updates are of course encrypted, from a secure DOT server.
