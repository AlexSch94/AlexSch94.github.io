Careers at DOT

At DOT, a small and dynamic team works on cutting-edge technologies in the field of digital devices in the logistics industry, with a focus on railway systems.

Our working culture encourages the individuality and creativity of each and every person in our strong team. In a start-up like environment, contributing your own ideas and shaping your job is engaging and fun.

The well-being of our employees is important to us - we provide flexible working hours, opportunities to learn new things and to grow on a personal and professional level, as well as a pleasant working environment, in a sustainable business. Working conditions are adapted to your needs, allowing for a healthy life-work balance.
