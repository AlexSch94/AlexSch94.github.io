Your chance to join a young and dynamic team in a leading telematics company. Apply now and help us shape the future!

Careers at DOT

At DOT, a small and dynamic team works on cutting-edge technologies in the field of digital devices in the logistics industry, with a focus on railway systems. Our working culture encourages individuality and creativity, providing an engaging and fun work experience, where you can contribute your ideas and shape your own job in a start-up like environment.

The well-being of our employees is important to us - we provide flexible working hours, opportunities to learn new things and to grow on a personal and professional level, as well as a pleasant working environment. Working conditions are adapted to your needs, allowing for a healthy life-work balance. This also means, that our requirements and expectations are flexible. In the case of interest in any of the offers below, please don't hesitate to contact us. We are looking forward to hearing from you!

What do we offer?

  • A diverse range of tasks in connection with the latest technologies.

  • The opportunity to help shape a young and dynamic team.

  • Workflows based on open communication and flat hierarchies.

  • Free lunch and a selection of drinks, cereal, and fresh fruits.

  • Creative forum and chill area in our green courtyard.

  • Team events.

  • A modern workplace in a green area, in an energy self-sufficient office building, reachable within 25 minutes from Vienna by express train.

  • Private parking.

  • The "Klimaticket", providing free, nationwide public transport for full-time employees.
